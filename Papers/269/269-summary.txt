This paper by Handel, Carlson, and Leitner, published in International Journal of Unconventional Electromagnetics and Plasmas, focuses on understanding the motion of ball lightning. The work synthesizes theoretical developments in the maser-caviton model to explain observed ball lightning trajectories, including translational motion, hovering behavior, passing through barriers, and interaction with electromagnetic fields, providing a unified framework for understanding ball lightning kinematics based on electromagnetic momentum transfer and plasma soliton dynamics.
