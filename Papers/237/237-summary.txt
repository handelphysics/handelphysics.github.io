This paper by Tournier and Handel examines quantum 1/f noise and quantum 1/f phase noise in relation to the uncertainty relations, presented at ICSSUR 2005. The research demonstrates fundamental connections between Heisenberg's uncertainty principle and the existence of 1/f noise in physical systems, showing that quantum mechanical constraints on simultaneous measurements of conjugate variables establish lower bounds on phase noise and frequency fluctuations in oscillators, sensors, and quantum devices.
