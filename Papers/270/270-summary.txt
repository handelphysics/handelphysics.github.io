This paper by Handel and George examines 1/f noise inside a Faraday cage, presented at the 20th International Conference on Noise and Fluctuations. The research investigates whether electromagnetic shielding affects quantum 1/f noise measurements, showing that while Faraday cages block external electromagnetic interference, they cannot eliminate fundamental quantum 1/f noise originating from intrinsic quantum mechanical processes within shielded devices, with implications for understanding the distinction between technical and fundamental noise sources in precision measurements.
