This paper by Handel, Li, and George examines quantum 1/f fluctuations in wind flow rate, submitted to Chaos, Solitons and Fractals. The work applies quantum 1/f noise theory beyond traditional electronic systems to macroscopic fluid dynamics, investigating whether fundamental quantum fluctuations contribute to observed 1/f spectral characteristics in atmospheric turbulence and wind velocity measurements, with implications for understanding scaling laws in turbulent flows, wind energy forecasting, and the connections between quantum mechanics and classical fluid dynamics.
